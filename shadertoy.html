About 4ljXD3

This page features the exact GLSL raytracing migration of the classic Shadertoy shader 4ljXD3 by jeantimex.

Shader Mechanics

The shader constructs a virtual 3D scene directly in the fragment shader:

  • Raytracing Loop: Traces camera rays and computes sphere intersections.
  • 4-Bounce Refraction: Lights refract inside a central glass sphere up to 4 times.
  • Atmospheric Sky: Procedural sky, sun dome, and horizon gradient.
  • Ground Floor: Procedural noise-mapping with fog attenuation.
  • Sub-spheres: A dynamic array of orbiting mini-spheres.

Interaction

Click and drag on the screen to control the camera. If you do not click, the camera will auto-rotate using a sine-wave path.
