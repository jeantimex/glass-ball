About

An interactive, physical glass shader simulation built with Three.js. Refraction, light scattering, and internal particle dynamics computed in real time.

Interactive Controls

Use the control panel in the top-right to fine-tune material settings like transmission, index of refraction (IOR), and particle speeds. Move your cursor to shift the internal light source.
